Strengths

Rather than focusing on your weaknesses, why not build upon your strengths? My top 5 talents are:

  • Input. I love to collect information - words, facts, books, quotations. It's interesting. It keeps my mind fresh
  • Achiever. Every day starts at zero. By the end of the day, I must achieve something tangible
  • Learner. I thrive on the steady and deliberate journey from ignorance to competence, the thrill of the first few facts, and the growing confidence of a skill mastered
  • Communication. I like to explain, to describe, to host, to speak in public, and to write. Ideas are a dry beginning. I need to bring them to life, to energize them, to make them exciting and vivid
  • Activator. "When can we start?" I am impatient for action. Analysis has its uses and discussion can occasionally yield some valuable insights, but only action is real
